Definitions:

• Safety Analysis Set: All subjects who received at least one dose (RFXSTDTC non-empty)

• TEAE: Treatment-Emergent Adverse Event (all AEs occurring after first dose)

• TRAE: Treatment-Related AE (AEREL_STD = 'POSSIBLY RELATED' or 'RELATED')

• Serious AE: AESER_STD = 'Y'

• DLT: Dose Limiting Toxicity (AEDLT_1_STD = 'Y')

• CTCAE Grade: 1=Mild, 2=Moderate, 3=Severe, 4=Life-threatening, 5=Death

• N = number of subjects in each dose group; n = subjects with at least one event; % = (n/N) × 100

• Each subject counted once per Preferred Term regardless of number of occurrences
