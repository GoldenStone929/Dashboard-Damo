ℹ️ Data Sources & Notes

Data Sources

  • Safety Analysis Set (N): Derived from DM (Demographics) domain - subjects with a valid first treatment date (RFXSTDTC).
  • Demographics (Sex, Age, Race, Ethnicity): Derived from DMMR (Demographics - Medical Record) domain, filtered by subjects present in VS (Vital Signs) data.
  • Anthropometric Measurements (Height, Weight, BMI, BSA): Derived from VS (Vital Signs) domain.

Why Numbers May Not Match

  • Different Data Sources: The Safety Analysis Set count comes from the DM domain, while sex/demographics counts come from the DMMR domain. Not all subjects may have records in both domains.
  • Missing Data: Some subjects may have incomplete demographic information (e.g., missing sex, race, or ethnicity values) recorded as blank, null, or "Unknown".
  • Data Filtering: Demographics are filtered to include only subjects with Vital Signs (VS) data available, which may exclude some subjects from the Safety Analysis Set.
